Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Card Catalog ("the App") is developed and published by Martin Creative Studios ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Summary

Card Catalog does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. Your library data is stored exclusively on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, in your personal iCloud account. We have no access to your data.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information.
  • We do not use analytics, crash-reporting services, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not track your usage of the App.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.

Data You Enter

All library data you create in Card Catalog — titles, authors, ratings, notes, and any other fields — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud Drive and iCloud sync enabled on your device, this data is also stored in your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. This sync is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

Camera Access

The App requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning barcodes (ISBN, UPC, and EAN codes) to look up media metadata. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or retained by the App. Camera access is optional — you can add items manually without using the camera.

Third-Party Metadata Services

When you scan a barcode or search for an item, the App queries publicly available metadata services (such as Open Library, Google Books, MusicBrainz, and iTunes Search) to retrieve information about that item. These queries include the barcode or search term you entered but do not include any personal information. These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Card Catalog is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@martinorganization.com.
