Card Catalog

Your entire collection — books, music, movies, and comics — beautifully organised in one place.

Available for iPhone, iPad & Mac

📚

Books, Music, Movies & Comics

One app for every type of media you collect. Each type has its own tailored fields and metadata sources.

📷

Barcode Scanning

Point your camera at any ISBN, UPC, or EAN barcode and Card Catalog looks up the metadata automatically from Amazon, Google Books, MusicBrainz, and more.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Your library lives in iCloud and stays in sync across all your Apple devices. Scan on iPhone, see it instantly on your Mac.

🔍

Smart Lists

Create saved searches that update automatically. Filter by 27 different fields — title, author, year, rating, condition, read status, and more.

📤

Import & Export

Import from Booxter, Delicious Library, Goodreads, LibraryThing, and CLZ. Export to CSV or HTML at any time.

🔒

Private by Design

No accounts, no ads, no tracking. Your collection data never leaves your devices and iCloud account.

📖

Lending Tracker

Mark items as lent out and record who has them. Never lose track of a borrowed book or movie again.

🛒

Buy on Amazon

Every item shows a direct link to its Amazon listing, so you can easily find a replacement copy or buy a gift for someone.

🖨️

Print Your Library

Print a formatted list of any collection or Smart List directly from the app — great for insurance records or sharing with friends.

Pricing

Card Catalog is free to download. You can catalog up to 50 items for free — no time limit, no credit card required. When your collection grows beyond 50 items, a one-time $29.99 Lifetime Unlock gives you unlimited items forever, on all your devices.

No subscriptions. No recurring fees. Pay once, own it forever.
